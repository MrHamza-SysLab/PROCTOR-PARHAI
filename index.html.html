ProctorParhai — Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 21, 2025

Introduction & Scope

ProctorParhai ("we", "us", "our") provides AI-powered assessments and proctoring services via proctorparhai.com and native mobile applications (collectively, the "Services"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you access or use our Services.

1. Information We Collect

Personal information

  • Full name, email address, phone number, username, password
  • Organization / institution details (if provided)

Assessment & proctoring data

  • Test answers, uploads, time-on-question, scores
  • Camera metadata (face detection, presence checks)
  • Microphone activity metadata (sound event detection)
  • Device information (model, OS, app version) and session logs

Note: we do not record or store full video/audio by default unless explicitly required or enabled by your institution. Proctoring captures events and metadata to detect suspicious activity.

2. How We Use Information

  • Deliver and manage assessments and proctoring
  • Verify identity and detect potential cheating
  • Provide automated grading, reports and personalized feedback
  • Improve Services and AI models using aggregated, anonymized data
  • Communicate updates, support messages, and administrative notices

3. Sharing & Disclosure

We only share data as necessary and under strict controls:

  • Institutions / employers: receive test results, reports and proctoring alerts when they administer the assessment.
  • Service providers: trusted vendors (cloud hosting, analytics) under confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal: to comply with legal requests or protect rights and safety.

4. Security

We implement industry-standard safeguards including encrypted transport (TLS), secure storage, role-based access controls, and regular security reviews. While we work to protect your data, no system is completely immune to risk.

5. Your Rights & Controls

  • Access and correct your personal data
  • Request deletion (subject to institutional retention requirements)
  • Withdraw consent for camera/microphone access (may prevent participation in proctored exams)
  • Request export of your data in a common format

To exercise any right, contact us at info@proctorparhai.com.

6. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to support the Services, comply with legal obligations, or meet institutional requirements. Typical retention practices:

  • Assessment records: retained per institution policy
  • Proctoring logs: up to 90 days by default (or as required)
  • Aggregated analytics: retained in anonymized form

7. Camera, Microphone & Proctoring Tools

During a proctored assessment, the app may request permission to access your camera and microphone. These permissions are used only to monitor the assessment session and are not used for general surveillance. You will be notified when proctoring starts and stopped.

8. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect or process personal data from children under the age of 13 without parental or institutional consent. If you believe we may have collected information from a child, contact us to remove it.

9. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to maintain sessions, remember preferences, and improve performance. You may block cookies in your browser, but some features may not function correctly.

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated via the Services or email. Continued use after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
